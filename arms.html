Arms Exercises

Bicep curls

The bicep curl targets the biceps brachii muscle. Hold a dumbbell in each hand and curl the weights towards your shoulders while keeping your elbows close to your body.

Hammer Curl

The hammer curl is similar to the bicep curl but with a neutral grip. Hold dumbbells with palms facing each other and curl the weights towards your shoulders.

Concentration Curl

The concentration curl isolates the biceps. Sit on a bench, rest your elbow against your inner thigh, and curl a dumbbell towards your shoulder.

Tricep Dip

The tricep dip targets the triceps brachii muscle. Use parallel bars or a bench, lower your body by bending your elbows, and then push back up.

Tricep Extensions

The tricep extension focuses on the triceps. Hold a dumbbell or a cable overhead with both hands and extend your arms upward.
